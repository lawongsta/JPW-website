Meet Dr. Justin Wong

Dr. Wong is an Arizona-native, born and raised in Flagstaff, Arizona. He graduated from University of Arizona with a degree in Pharmacy and became board certified in Arizona and California, but continued on to graduate dental school, class of 1982 from University of Southern California.

After graduating, Dr. Wong returned to Tucson to practice dentistry, where he has been for 38 years with 31 years at this location. Dr. Wong established his own practice and has been in his current office for 21 years.

Dr. Wong has two daughters, Kelsey and Lauren and a wife, Lisa who is a pharmacist in Tucson. His interests are vast, as he loves to try everything. He enjoys the outdoors and playing golf, tennis, fishing, skiing and basketball. Indoor hobbies include working with wood and metal.
Dr. Wong is a member in good standing with the following professional organizations:
American Dental Association, Arizona Dental Association
